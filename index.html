Why Choose SeedSafe?

Everything you need for ironclad security.

100% Offline

Your data never leaves your device. No servers, no clouds, no internet connection required. Pure privacy.

Ironclad Encryption

Using open-source, industry-standard algorithms (AES-256-GCM & PBKDF2) to lock your vault.

Biometric Unlock

Access your vault in a split second using your fingerprint or face. Secure, fast, and convenient.

Open Source

Trust through transparency. Our code is open for the world to see, audit, and verify. No hidden surprises.

Encrypted Export

Backup your entire vault to a single encrypted QR code or a `.ssv` file. Your backup is as secure as your vault.

No Vendor Lock-in

Your data is yours. Use our open-source web tool to recover your phrases even if you don't have the app.

Trust Through Transparency

You Are in Complete Control.

SeedSafe is built on a simple principle: we believe you should have absolute ownership of your data. We never have access to your keys, your password, or your information.

  • ✓
    Non-Custodial by Design

    Your vault is stored only on your device, not on our servers. If you lose your device without a backup, your data is gone forever. That's true security.

  • ✓
    Open Source Standards

    We use battle-tested, open-source cryptographic algorithms like AES-256-GCM. No proprietary code, no hidden backdoors. You can even use our web recovery tool to decrypt your data without our app.

  • ✓
    Zero Tracking or Analytics

    The app contains no trackers, no analytics, and no monitoring tools. Your activity is your business, and nobody else's.
