Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025-08-18

SeedSafe is designed to work 100% offline. This website and the Offline Recovery Tool do not send your data to any server.

What We Do Not Collect

  • No analytics or tracking cookies

Offline Recovery Tool

Decryption happens locally in your browser. Files you open never leave your device. Close the page/tab after use.
