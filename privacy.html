Privacy Policy – SeedSafe

Last updated: 22 August 2025

SeedSafe (the “App”) is an offline password & sensitive notes manager by Jenova (“we”, “our”, “us”). This policy explains how the App processes data on your device.

Scope

SeedSafe does not require an account and does not connect to our servers. All processing happens on your device.

Data We Collect

We do not collect or transmit your personal data to any server. All data (passwords, notes, recovery phrases) is stored only on your device.

On‑device Processing & Security

  • Data is stored encrypted on the device.
  • Access is protected by a passcode and/or biometrics (where available).
  • We do not have access to your encryption keys and cannot recover data if you forget your passcode.

Permissions Used

  • Biometrics: to unlock the app.
  • Storage / Files (scoped): for user‑initiated export/import of files.
  • Camera (if enabled): to scan/show QR codes for export/import.

The App does not display ads.

Data Sharing

We do not share your data with third parties.

Retention & User Control

Your data remains until you delete it, clear the app’s data, or uninstall the app.

Data Deletion

There is no server‑side account. To delete data, you can clear the app’s storage or uninstall the app: Android → Settings → Apps → SeedSafe → Storage → Clear Storage.

Children

The App is intended for users 18+.

Limitation of Liability

All data in SeedSafe is stored locally on your device. We do not have access to your passcode, encryption keys, or stored data. If your device is lost, damaged, restored, or you forget the passcode, we cannot help recover your data. You are solely responsible for maintaining backups/exports and safeguarding your data.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy and will update the date above accordingly.
